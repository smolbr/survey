Fe del Mundo

The first pediatrician in the Philippines.

This is her life and achievements

    1911
  • She was born in November 27 at Intramuros, Manila.
    • 1933
  • She earned her medical degree at University of the Philippines.
    • 1939
  • She went in Harvard Medical School's Children's Hospital for a two year research fellowship
    • 1941
  • She returned to the Philippines, joined the International Red Cross and volunteered to care for child-internees.
    • 1957
  • She established the Children's Medical Center, the first pediatric hospital in the Philippines
    • 1957 onward
  • She continued to provide medical care for Filipinos, especially children, in rural areas until her death in 2011.
